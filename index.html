Prof. Dr. Ing. Rozenn DAHYOT

Associate Professor in Statistics

I am currently an Associate Professor in Statistics in the School of Computer Science and Statistics in Trinity College Dublin Ireland. I am a member of the ADAPT research Centre for Digital Content Technology focuses on developing next generation digital technologies and regroup researchers accross seven leading Irish Higher Education Institutions: Maynooth University, Trinity College Dublin, Dublin City University, University College Dublin, Technological University Dublin, Munster Technological University and Athlone Institute of Technology. I am leading a research team focusing on data analysis & visualisation. I have served as secretary and president of the Irish Pattern Recognition and Classification Society (IPRCS) (2008-2020) and was its representative on the International Association for Pattern Recognition (IAPR). Since 2005, I have been teaching on topics in Mathematics & Statistics. I also served as tutor for the College in the period 2009-2018. I currently lead the effort for organising the European Signal Processing Conference EUSIPCO2021 in Dublin in 2021 (August 23-27th 2021).

For more information, see my Research Overview, my LinkedIn CV, my publications or Google Scholar profile.

Update: Check TCD official website for updated information. I currently work from my home office and I can be contacted via email (rozenn.dahyot @ tcd.ie). E-meetings can be organised (e.g. with Skype for Business, Teams, Zoom, etc.).
