Prof. Dr. Ing. Rozenn DAHYOT

Associate Professor in Statistics

I am an Associate Professor in Statistics in the School of Computer Science and Statistics in Trinity College Dublin Ireland, and I will be joining Maynooth University as Professor of Computer Science from April 2021. I am a member of the ADAPT research Centre for Digital Content Technology that focuses on developing next generation digital technologies and regroups researchers accross several leading Irish Higher Education Institutions. I lead the effort for organising the European Signal Processing Conference EUSIPCO2021 in Dublin in 2021 (August 23-27th 2021).

The website for the European Conference on Signal Processing 2021 in Dublin is slowly emerging at https://t.co/gAi2ShZs9S
where you can register your interest - Follow us @eusipco2021 and spread the word #eusipco2021 ! pic.twitter.com/0U5g6qRrJx

— eusipco2021 (@eusipco2021) November 19, 2019

My research interests are in between the traditional fields of Electronic and electrical engineering (digital signal processing), Computer Science (computer vision & graphics), artificial intelligence (data mining, machine learning) and Mathematics (Statistics, Information theory, decision theory, functional analysis).
